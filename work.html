Natalia’s Creations

I transformed my passion for pastry-making and cake decorating into a successful home-based business specializing in customized cakes for children’s birthdays, community gatherings, and local events. Through this entrepreneurial venture, I gained valuable experience in time management, financial accounting, client relations, and business operations. Starting my own business early in my career taught me how to effectively balance professional responsibilities, academics, and extracurricular activities—skills that continue to support my personal and professional growth.

TedEd Talk

In 2022, I had the privilege of delivering a TED Talk focused on the transformative power of inclusive education—a topic deeply inspired by my personal experiences in high school. In this presentation, I emphasized the critical importance of adopting student-centered educational practices that nurture the holistic development and well-being of every individual. This milestone allowed me to refine my public speaking skills and strengthened my commitment to advocating for meaningful educational change. Now, as a university student, I continue to champion inclusivity and holistic approaches to learning, driven by the insights and passion developed through this impactful experience.
